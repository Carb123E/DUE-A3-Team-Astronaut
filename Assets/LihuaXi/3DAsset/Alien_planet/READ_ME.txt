Thank you for purchasing Aline Planet pack:

The 4 demo scenes are setup with colliders and effects, just add a character controller into the scenes and you are ready to go.
The props and terrains are using a single mobile shader.
There is a prefab folder for your convenience.
The terrain slabs are tilable in every directions.
The assets are divided into 4 different themes (Lush, Dry, Gas and Ice)
There are 4 different skyboxes, one for each theme.
